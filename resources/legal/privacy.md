# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Tread·Mark collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We have written it to comply with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, **California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA)**, **Canada's PIPEDA**, and other applicable privacy laws.

---

## 1. Who Is Responsible for Your Data

**Data controller:** Steven Tringali, operating Tread·Mark.
**Contact:** [hello@treadmark.app](mailto:hello@treadmark.app)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or Switzerland, we are the data controller for your personal information.

---

## 2. What We Collect

### Information you provide directly

| Data | Why we collect it |
|---|---|
| First name, last name | Personalize your account |
| Email address | Account login, transactional emails (verification, password reset) |
| Vehicle information (year, make, model, tire count) | Core app functionality |
| Tire data (labels, tread readings, rotation notes, odometer) | Core app functionality |

### Information collected automatically

| Data | Why we collect it |
|---|---|
| IP address | Security (rate limiting, abuse prevention) |
| Browser type and version | Diagnosing technical issues |
| Pages visited and timestamps | Understanding how the app is used |

We do **not** use third-party analytics trackers (e.g., Google Analytics). Any usage data is collected server-side and used only for service improvement and security.

### Cookies

We use only **essential session cookies** required for authentication and CSRF protection. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. No cookie consent banner is required for these.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

- Provide, operate, and improve the Service.
- Send transactional emails (account verification, password reset). We will not send marketing email without your explicit opt-in.
- Monitor for security incidents and prevent abuse.
- Comply with legal obligations.

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes — ever.**

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we process your data under the following lawful bases:

| Processing activity | Legal basis |
|---|---|
| Providing the Service (account, vehicle, tire data) | **Contract** — necessary to perform the service you signed up for |

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all associated data (profile, vehicles, tires, rotations, placements) is permanently deleted within **30 days**.

Server logs containing IP addresses are retained for up to **90 days** for security purposes and then deleted.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the **United States** (hosted on [Laravel Cloud](https://cloud.laravel.com)). If you are based outside the US, your data is transferred internationally. For EEA/UK users, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards as required.

We use industry-standard safeguards including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), hashed passwords (bcrypt), and database-level access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### All users

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Correction** — update inaccurate or incomplete data (most data is editable in-app).
- **Deletion** — delete your account and all associated data from your profile settings page.

### EEA / UK / Switzerland (GDPR)

In addition to the above:

- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
- **Restriction** — request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
- **Objection** — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.
- **Lodge a complaint** — you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, the CNIL in France).

### California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

- Know what personal information we collect and how it is used.
- Delete personal information (subject to certain exceptions).
- Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information — **we do not sell or share your personal information**.
- Non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [hello@treadmark.app](mailto:hello@treadmark.app). We will respond within 30 days (or within the timeframe required by applicable law).

### Canadian users (PIPEDA)

You have the right to access your personal information and to challenge its accuracy. Contact us at [hello@treadmark.app](mailto:hello@treadmark.app).
